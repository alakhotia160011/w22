HOW TO USE THE TERMINAL

W22 is a Bloomberg-style terminal for tracking markets, reading news, and analysing securities. Everything is driven by the command bar at the top. Type a command and press Enter.

GETTING STARTED
Navigate homeClick W22 logo (top left)
Run a commandType in command bar, press Enter
Search for a tickerStart typing a company name - suggestions appear
Load a securityType ticker (e.g. AAPL) or name (e.g. apple)
MARKET SCREENS
WWatchlist - your personal worksheet with live quotes, editable buy prices, RSI, charts. Click any ticker for a chart. Add/delete rows. Edit buy prices inline.
WEIWorld Equity Indices - S&P 500, Dow, NASDAQ, FTSE, DAX, Nikkei, Hang Seng, and more. Click any index for a chart.
WEIFEquity Index Futures - E-mini S&P, NASDAQ, Dow, Russell, Nikkei futures. Live prices, especially useful pre-market.
FXForeign Exchange - G10 pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY...), EM currencies, DXY index, Bitcoin & Ethereum.
GLCOGlobal Commodities - WTI, Brent, Natural Gas, Gold, Silver, Copper, Corn, Wheat, Coffee, and more.
FITFixed Income - US Treasury yield curve (3M, 5Y, 10Y, 30Y), treasury futures, treasury ETFs.
HEATSector Heatmap - visual map of S&P 500 or NASDAQ 100 by sector. Toggle 1D/5D/1M/3M. Click any stock for a chart.
SECURITY ANALYSIS

First load a security by typing its ticker or name. Then use these sub-commands:

GPPrice chart - 1D to MAX history. Toggle $ price vs normalised (base 100). Add comparison tickers with the COMPARE input.
DESCompany description - sector, industry, HQ, employee count, website, market cap.
FAFundamental analysis - valuation (P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA), profitability (margins, ROE), balance sheet. Tabs for Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow.
EVTEvents - next earnings date, dividend dates, 8 quarters of EPS history with beat/miss tracking.
CNCompany news - latest headlines from Yahoo Finance for the loaded ticker.
COMPPeer comparison - benchmarks the loaded stock against industry peers across 14 financial metrics (P/E, margins, growth, etc).
CACSCorporate actions - dividend history, stock splits, upcoming ex-dividend and pay dates, payout ratio.
MEMBETF holdings - top 10 holdings with weights, sector breakdown, fund info, expense ratio. Works only for ETFs (IGV, SPY, QQQ...).

You can also go direct: FA NVDA, COMP AAPL, MEMB IGV

RATES & ECONOMICS
WIRPRate expectations - how the market is pricing Fed and BoJ rate cuts/hikes at each meeting. Uses fed funds and TONA futures.
ECOEconomic calendar - this week's US releases (CPI, NFP, GDP, ISM...). Filter by impact. Surprise index tracks beats vs misses.
ANALYSIS TOOLS
HS T1 T2Historical Spread - compare 2 tickers side by side. Top chart: normalised or $ price. Bottom chart: subtract, ratio, or residual. Use the flip button to swap.
PREDPrediction markets from Polymarket. Browse by category (Geopolitics, Politics, Crypto, Sports...). Click any market for price history chart.
MOSTTop movers - day's biggest gainers, losers, and most active stocks in a 3-column view. Click any for a chart.
SRCHEquity screener - filter stocks by market cap, P/E, sector. Uses Finviz data. Click any result for a chart.
NEWS & READING
TOPAggregated news from DealBook, FT, WSJ, Reuters, CNBC, BBC, Axios, MarketWatch. Filter by source. Click headlines to read full articles.
NEWSYour email newsletters - Money Stuff, DealBook, TLDR, Endpoints, GZERO, The Athletic, LatAm Briefing. Pulled directly from your Gmail inbox.
OTHER
CHATAI chatbot powered by Claude. Ask any question - markets, finance, general knowledge. Has live market data in context.
